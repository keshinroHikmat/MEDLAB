MEDILAB

A medical laboratory (often abbreviated as medilab) is a specialized facility that performs diagnostic tests and analyses on biological samples, such as blood, urine, tissue, and other bodily fluids. These laboratories play a critical role in healthcare by helping diagnose diseases, monitor treatment effectiveness, and support research and development in medicine.
