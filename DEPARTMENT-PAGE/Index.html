Departments

Departments at Our Medlab

Cardiology

Cardiology Department The Cardiology Department specializes in diagnosing, treating, and preventing disorders of the heart and blood vessels. This includes a range of cardiovascular diseases such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and congenital heart defects. *Diagnostic Tests: • Electrocardiogram (ECG or EKG) • Echocardiography • Stress testing • Holter monitoring • Cardiac catheterization and angiography

Neurology

Neurology Department The Neurology Department focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, and muscles. This includes a wide range of neurological conditions such as strokes, epilepsy, migraines, and neurodegenerative diseases. *Diagnostic Tests: • Electroencephalogram (EEG) • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) and Computed Tomography (CT) • Nerve conduction studies (NCS) and Electromyography (EMG) • Lumbar puncture for cerebrospinal fluid analysis

Hepatology

Neurology Department The Neurology Department focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, and muscles. This includes a wide range of neurological conditions such as strokes, epilepsy, migraines, and neurodegenerative diseases. Treatment and Management: • Stroke care and rehabilitation • Seizure and epilepsy management • Treatment of movement disorders like Parkinsons disease • Chronic headache and migraine therapy • Multiple sclerosis and autoimmune disorder treatment * Specialized Care: • Management of neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimers and ALS • Pain management for neuropathy and neuralgia • Sleep disorder evaluations and treatment

Pediatrics

Pediatrics Department The Pediatrics Department specializes in the medical care and treatment of infants, children, and adolescents, ensuring their physical, mental, and emotional well-being from birth to young adulthood. It addresses a wide range of conditions, from routine health checkups to complex medical issues. * Preventive Care: • Routine health checkups and growth monitoring • Immunizations and vaccinations • Nutritional guidance and developmental assessments • Health education and counseling for parents * Specialized Pediatric Services: • Pediatric surgery and emergency care • Pediatric cardiology, neurology, and gastroenterology consultations • Care for genetic and congenital disorders • Pediatric oncology and hematology services

Eye Care

Eye Care Department The Eye Care Department focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of eye and vision-related conditions. It offers a wide range of services, from routine eye exams to advanced surgical procedures, ensuring optimal eye health and clear vision for patients of all ages. * Routine Eye Care: • Comprehensive eye exams • Vision testing and prescription of glasses or contact lenses • Screening for common conditions like cataracts, glaucoma, and macular degeneration * Medical and Surgical Treatment: • Management of refractive errors (myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism) • Treatment of eye infections, allergies, and dry eye syndrome • Cataract surgery, LASIK, and other corrective procedures • Glaucoma management, including medication and surgery • Retinal treatments for conditions such as diabetic retinopathy
